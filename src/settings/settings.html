How to find the IP Address

OS X / Windows:

  • Launch the Sonos Controller App
  • Click on Sonos > "About my Sonos System"
  • The IP Address is in the big white box, after "Associated ZP:"

iOS / Android:

  • Launch the Sonos App
  • Scroll all the way down, choose Settings
  • Choose "About my Sonos System"
  • The IP Address is in the big white box, after "Associated ZP:"
